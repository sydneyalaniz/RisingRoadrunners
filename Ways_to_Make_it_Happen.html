Ways to Make it Happen

There are a lot of factors to consider, but this project is not impossible. We could start off the program by partnering with high schools in rural areas or low-income areas to advertise and encourage the project. A good next step could be an assessment to evaluate what resources are vital to underprivileged students to success at our university. We have many mentorship programs on-campus that we could collaborate with to help start the program or at least help figure out what trainings are needed. Something to take into consideration is that the project is a good opportunity for a student work-study position. It should not be hard to find participants. This program would be a lot of work, but it is not impossible.
