Roadrunner Mentors

A peer mentorship program aimed to facilitate access to higher education for underprivileged students that are envisioning themselves at the University of Texas at San Antonio. This program would pair an experienced roadrunner student with a student from a disadvantaged background to offer guidance and support through the roadrunner application process. It would allow incoming roadrunners to be informed about the resources the university has to offer. To conclude, the vision for the program is to empower and guide future roadrunners to success, no matter their background.
