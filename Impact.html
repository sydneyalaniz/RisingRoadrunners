Impact

Implementing a peer mentorship program for underprivileged students to succeed at our university would allow our community to foster an inclusive and diverse campus. It would allow our community to become more empathetic and give these disadvantage individuals a chance to succeed at out university. It would also influence a wide range of backgrounds and perspectives to be represented in the student body. These students would give our community a beautiful, unique, and vibrant atmosphere. It would allow these students to feel seen and heard in our community.
